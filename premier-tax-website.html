About Premier Tax & Accounting

Premier Tax & Accounting Services Inc. is a professional tax and accounting firm located in Jackson Heights, New York. We provide a wide range of financial services to individuals and businesses, including tax preparation, accounting, payroll processing, and immigration form assistance.

Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering personalized service and expert advice to help our clients achieve their financial goals. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, integrity, and commitment to excellence.
